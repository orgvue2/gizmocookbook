## Supported operators

#### Arithematical operators
**+** (addition)

**-** (subtraction)

***** (multiplication)

**/** (division)

**%** (modulus)

****** (power)

#### Bitwise operators
**&** (and)

**|** (or)

**^** (xor)

#### Logical operators
**AND** or **&&**

**OR** or **||**

**NOT** or **!**

#### Comparison operators
**==** (equal)

**===** (identical)

**!=** (not equal)

**!==** (not identical)

**<** (less than)

**>** (greater than)

**<=** (less than or equal to)

**>=** (greater than or equal to)

#### Numeric operators
**..** (range)

#### Conditional ternary operators
**foo ? 'yes' : 'no'** (If “foo” then “yes” else “no”)
